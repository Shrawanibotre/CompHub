EVENTS

Within our department, a myriad of events ranging from technical competitions like coding challenges to recreational activities such as sports tournaments were organized, fostering a vibrant and dynamic environment for skill development and camaraderie.

Industry Visit

Our industrial visit to Bajaj was an invaluable experience, providing us with practical insights into manufacturing processes. Witnessing the assembly lines in action helped us understand the intricacies of mass production firsthand. It was a remarkable opportunity to bridge the gap between theory and practice, enhancing our understanding of industrial operations.

Technical Event

Hosting technical events like "Need for Speed" at a national level certification platform was exhilarating. It provided participants with a platform to showcase their skills and innovations in a competitive environment. Such events not only foster healthy competition but also contribute significantly to the advancement of technical knowledge and expertise on a broader scale.

Social Event

Our visit to the orphanage was heartwarming as we engaged in teaching the kids about technology. Through interactive sessions, we shared our knowledge and sparked their curiosity about the digital world. It was a fulfilling experience witnessing their eagerness to learn and making a positive impact through social activity.

Sports Event

Participating in sports like cricket, volleyball, and kabaddi instilled a sense of camaraderie and teamwork among us. These activities not only promoted physical fitness but also taught valuable lessons in coordination and strategy. Engaging in diverse sports helped us develop a well-rounded skill set and fostered a spirit of healthy competition within our community.
